Zendy Video Resizer automatically resizes embedded videos from YouTube, Vimeo, or Wistia to fit the full width of your content.

== Description ==
Zendy Video Resizer automatically resizes embedded videos from YouTube, Vimeo, or Wistia to fit the full width of your content and also sets the height of the video to fit 16:9 ratio of HD videos.

== Installation ==
1. Upload the `zendy-video-resizer` folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` menu in WordPress
3. That's it, Enjoy!

== Frequently Asked Questions ==
= What does Zendy Video Resizer do? =

Zendy Video Resizer looks for embedded code from YouTube, Vimeo, or Wistia (iframe tags) and resizes the video to fit the full width of your theme layout while keeping a 16:9 aspect ratio.

= Does Zendy Video Resizer work with video services other than YouTube, Vimeo, or Wistia? =

Only YouTube, Vimeo, and Wistia are supported at this time. Please open a support request if you would like other video services to be added.
